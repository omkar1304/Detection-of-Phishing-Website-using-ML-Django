Integrity

Integrity is basically refers to methods of ensuring that data is real, accurate and safeguarded from unauthorized user modification.

Authorization

Authorization is a security mechanism to determine access levels or user/client privileges related to system resources including urls,files.

Availability

Availability means that information and service is always accessible to authorized users & can be accessed by authenticated users whenever they're needed.

Confidentiality refers to protecting information from being accessed by unauthorized parties or let's say from hackers,your data can be access by you only.
